Overview

Medora Auri Family Beach Resort is a modern 4-star hotel, situated in the very heart of Makarska Riviera, in a quiet and picturesque village Podgora. Just at this point the powerful Biokovo connects with the sea, all the colours of this Dalmatian landscape come together and all its natural contrasts that encourage the personal revival of each guest thus contributing to the strongest authentic experience of the destination.
 
Hotel Medora Aura is part of the largest hotel complex in Podgora from whose centre is only 300 meters away. Opened in July 2016, after complete renovation and reconstruction, thanks to the top-quality accommodation and ideal location right next to the beach, today hotel is the starting point for numerous activities. Activities include exploring the adjacent golden-blue Adriatic coast, stunning green and silver nature and invite to discover rich cultural heritage and folk customs.

Visit Medora Auri hotel and make sure that Podgora is the place of your personal revival.
